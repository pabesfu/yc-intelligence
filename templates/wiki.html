How to Apply to Y Combinator

YC accepts ~1% of applicants (~70-80K applications/year). The application is short but every word matters.

What works

  • Clear one-liner under 50 chars
  • Measurable traction (revenue, users, growth %)
  • Domain expertise proof
  • Technical co-founder with shared history
  • Authentic 1-min video (no scripts)

Common mistakes

  • Buzzword-heavy descriptions
  • No traction data or vague metrics
  • Overly produced founder video
  • TAM from Google search
  • Feature list instead of insight
