How to Apply to Y Combinator

YC accepts ~1% of applicants (~70-80K applications/year). The application is short but every word matters. Here's what we've learned from analyzing thousands of successful applications.

What works
  • Clear, specific one-liner (under 50 chars)
  • Measurable traction (revenue, users, growth %)
  • Domain expertise proof ("I worked 10 years in...")
  • Technical co-founder with shared history
  • Authentic 1-min video (no scripts, no polish)
Common mistakes
  • Buzzword-heavy descriptions ("AI-powered synergy")
  • No traction data or vague metrics
  • Overly produced founder video
  • TAM from Google search ("$400B market")
  • Feature list instead of insight/thesis
